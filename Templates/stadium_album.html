Cactus League Stadiums

We captured images of all 9 spring training facilities in Arizona.
For directions please refer to the map at the bottom of the page.

Salt River Fields. Home of the Arizona Diamondbacks and Colorado Rockies.

Scottsdale Stadium. Home to the San Francisco Giants.

Tempe Diablo Stadium. Home to the Los Angeles Angels.

Goodyear Stadium. Home to the Cleveland Indians and Cincinatti Reds.

Surprise Stadium. Home of the Kansas City Royals and Texas Rangers.

Camelback Ranch. Home of the Los Angeles Dodgers and Chicago White Sox.

Peoria Sports Complex. Home of the Seattle Mariners and San Diego Padres.

Ho Ho Kam Stadium. Home to the Oakland Athletics.

Sloan Park (AKA WrigleyVille West). Home to the Chicago Cubs.
